About Us

Located on El Cajon Boulevard, College Billiards is committed to the community and offering a place for residents around it to relax, reconnect, and enjoy the fun game of pool and an ice cold beer. With plenty of table and space inside, there's always room for one more, so come on and join us sometime!

Hungry? We got you covered! There's no need to eat before you come. Just bring your wallet and your appetite, and our chef will take fine care of you!
